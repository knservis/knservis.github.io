## Patient profiles

Adults and teenagers facing a range of issues across their lifespan such as:

- difficulties linked to emotions (painful emotions, inhibition, stress, anxiety)
- problems linked to behaviour (addictions, OCD, phobias)
- troubles related to difficult events (trauma, break-ups, general uneasiness, bereavements, painful experiences in family relationships or in childhood)
- relational or attachment difficulties (couple, relationships with others, work)
- difficult life period or wish for change (mourning, change of position, separation, illness, serious illness of a loved one, expatriation, birth, etc.)
- existential or identity questions

## Therapeutical approach

- in-depth therapies, such as psychodynamic and psychoanalytic psychotherapy and couple & family therapies
- references to other techniques: group therapy, transcultural therapy, emotion-centred therapy, transactional analysis
- standardised tests
